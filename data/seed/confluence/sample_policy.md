# Employee Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.

## Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

## Restricted Access
> **Allowed Groups:** HR, Management
> **Note:** Managers can approve up to 5 additional days of unpaid leave per year.
